Detailed Bonzai Tree Information

Japanese Maple

The Japanese Maple is celebrated for its vibrant red leaves and elegant structure. This tree thrives in partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. It's perfect for those who appreciate a striking centerpiece.

Juniper Bonzai

The Juniper Bonzai is a classic evergreen with a hardy nature, making it ideal for beginners. It thrives in full sunlight and requires minimal pruning, ensuring a low-maintenance addition to any space.

Ficus Bonzai

With lush green foliage, the Ficus Bonzai brings a tropical vibe to your home or office. This tree loves indirect sunlight and benefits from regular misting to keep its leaves fresh and healthy.
