Bonzai Care Tips

Tree Type Watering Frequency Sunlight Needs
Japanese Maple Twice a week Partial shade
Juniper Bonzai Weekly Full sun
Ficus Bonzai Every 3 days Indirect sunlight
Chinese Elm Every 4 days Partial sun

Steps to Start Growing a Bonzai

  1. Choose the right tree species.
  2. Pick a suitable pot and soil.
  3. Prune and shape your tree.
  4. Provide proper watering and sunlight.
  5. Repot periodically to encourage growth.
